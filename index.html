🪴 Where the site is going

The plan is to make this feel less like a pile of uploads and more like a living garden journal with practical tools underneath it.

Plant Progress
The main storytelling section. Each plant gets an earthtone journal page with multiple dated entries over the season, instead of a brand new page for every single photo.
Garden Calendar
The practical side of the garden, seasonal tasks, timing, applications, and reminders tied to this actual yard in Philadelphia.
Bed and planning pages
Some pages will track full beds, layout ideas, before-and-after decisions, and experiments when a single-plant page is not the right fit.
Seasonal archives later
As photos build up, the site can add Spring, Summer, and Fall archive views so you can browse the garden by season as well as by plant.
Photos stay lighter
The Photos section can stay more casual, while Plant Progress becomes the polished record of how the yard changes over time.
