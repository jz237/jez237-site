Seasonal Storytelling

Plant Progress

A warm, month-by-month journal for the plants that make the yard feel alive.

How it should feel

Not just a gallery. More like a living field journal, where each plant gets its own little chapter and every two weeks or so becomes part of the season’s story.

bud break first bloom leaf color stress + recovery late-season glow

Update rhythm

Every plant page can take entries weekly, every two weeks, or just whenever you’ve got a photo worth keeping. The structure still reads cleanly.

Suggested photo path: photos/garden/plants/<slug>/YYYY/MM/

Suggested note fields: date, caption, what changed, what you did, what to watch next.
