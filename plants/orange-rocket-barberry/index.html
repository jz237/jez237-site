Orange Rocket Barberry

A narrow upright shrub with spring color already coming in hot, reading more ember than green even before the season really gets moving.

Color first, structure second

Orange Rocket earns its place fast because it does not wait around to disappear into the background. Even in early spring it is already carrying that red-orange tone that makes it read like a lit vertical accent against mulch, stone edging, and lawn.
